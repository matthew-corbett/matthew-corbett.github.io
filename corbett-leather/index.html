Our Story

Made for Makers, by Makers

Corbett & Company Leather Works started at a workbench, not a boardroom. As a veteran-owned shop, we build the gear we wanted but couldn't find: honest, full-grain leather goods that are tough enough for the range and refined enough for the road.

From handmade travel bags and Dopp kits to custom holsters and the 3D-printed tools we use to make them, everything we sell is something we'd carry ourselves. When you buy from us, you're supporting American makers and getting a piece built to be passed down.
