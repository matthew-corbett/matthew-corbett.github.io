About

I am Research Division Chief for AI Operations at the Army Cyber Institute, United States Military Academy, West Point. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Virginia Tech in 2024, advised by Dr. Bo Ji in the SNAIL lab.

My work spans peer-reviewed systems research in augmented reality privacy, AI-driven cyber conflict simulation, and operational studies of command-and-control decision-making in AI-enabled military environments.
